made for what lasts.

Lineage Films makes cinematic films and documentaries for aligned brands, businesses, founders and individuals.

In an age of generated everything, we focus on capturing what’s actually there. We find stories, nurture them, and shape them into films that hold weight. There’s nothing as timeless as real people, real places, and real passion. Films that carry forward.
