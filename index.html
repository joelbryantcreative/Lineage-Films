01 / Approach

made for what lasts.

Lineage Films makes cinematic films for people doing real work in right relationship with land, body, and tradition. We embed with operators building something worth passing on — and shape what we find into films that hold their weight.

In an age of generated everything, we make films that were actually there. Real people. Real places. Real practices. Films that carry forward.
